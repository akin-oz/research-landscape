## Citation and evidence rule

Faculty reports cite these stable labels. A source index can establish identity, topic, date, venue, or a reported metric; it does **not** establish programming language, code availability, mentoring quality, or supervisory availability unless it says so. Negative findings in this pilot mean “not found in the cited public search window,” never “does not exist.”
