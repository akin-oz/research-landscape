# Adem Tekin — ITU Computational Science and Engineering

## Evidence profile

Tekin is a current ITU professor in the Informatics Institute's Computational Science and Engineering environment. His group explicitly covers computational materials design, crystal-structure prediction, energy materials, global optimization, and the massively parallel FFCASP algorithm. [ITU profile](https://akademi.itu.edu.tr/en/tekinad/Adem-Tekin/) · [group site](https://web.itu.edu.tr/~tekinad/)

The 2026 PNcsp+ study is unusually direct evidence for this candidate profile: it is an open-source Python crystal-structure-prediction implementation, documented in the paper, with materials-data/ML-force-field connections. Recent outputs include ML discovery/screening of perovskites and DFT energy-materials work. Course material includes Linux, shell, make, AWK, gnuplot, Fortran/C, and scripting for science/engineering projects. Official thesis records show completed and ongoing computational screening work. [PNcsp+ paper/code](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC13085239/) · [publications](https://web.itu.edu.tr/~tekinad/?page_id=64) · [software course](https://ninova.itu.edu.tr/en/courses/institute-of-informatics/13508/hbm-801/form) · [theses](https://akademi.itu.edu.tr/tekinad/Tezler)
