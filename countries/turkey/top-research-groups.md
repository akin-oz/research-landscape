# Top research environments for this trajectory

These are research environments, not institutional rankings. The order reflects fit to software-enabled computational materials and the evidence available in the nationwide search.

1. **ITU Computational Science & Engineering / Adem Tekin group** — crystal prediction, computational materials design, HPC, and public Python research software. [Dossier](istanbul-technical-university/faculty/adem-tekin.md)
2. **Koç NEMO / Seda Keskin Avcı** — MOF databases, molecular simulation, ML, public data/code, and ERC-scale research. [Dossier](koc-university/faculty/seda-keskin-avci.md)
3. **METU Computational Materials Science Group / Hande Toffoli** — DFT/MD, direct computational-materials curriculum, active graduate cohort. [Dossier](middle-east-technical-university/faculty/hande-toffoli.md)
4. **Bilkent UNAM/MSN** — a multi-advisor computational-materials cluster with Durgun and Jahangirov, graduate methods teaching, and documented European alumni paths. [Dossiers](bilkent-university/faculty/)
5. **Sabancı Materials Science and Nano Engineering / Ongun Özçelik** — active first-principles energy/materials projects and direct MSc computational projects. [Dossier](sabanci-university/faculty/ongun-ozcelik.md)
6. **ITU Materials/Surface environment / Cem Örnek** — experimental and computational integration for hydrogen, corrosion, coatings, and degradation. [Dossier](istanbul-technical-university/faculty/cem-ornek.md)
7. **METU Chemistry / Erol Yıldırım** — polymer/nanocomposite simulations, ML+DFT, GROMACS/HPC learning signal, and experimental collaboration. [Dossier](middle-east-technical-university/faculty/erol-yildirim.md)
8. **Akdeniz MSE / Uğurlu–Bal bridge** — emerging coatings-materials ML with public research code; capacity and data terms need confirmation. [Dossier](akdeniz-university/materials-engineering/faculty/sadettin-yavuz-ugurlu.md)

## Exclusions

Institutions or departments with only generic AI, generic materials, or unverified computational claims were not promoted. Historical pages were not treated as evidence of a current advisor appointment.
