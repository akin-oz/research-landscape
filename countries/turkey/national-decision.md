## Practical application strategy

Apply first to **Tekin, Keskin Avcı, Uğurlu, Toffoli, Durgun, Sargın, and Yıldırım**. Use a two-track strategy:

1. **Materials-informatics / data / scientific-software:** Tekin, Keskin Avcı, Uğurlu, Sargın, Taşcı, Aydın.
2. **DFT/MD / multiscale computational materials:** Toffoli, Durgun, Yıldırım, Yurtsever, Örnek, Özçelik, Kılıç, Kırca, Aryanfar, Dansuk, Özdoğan, Aral.

For every contact, propose a thesis with two inseparable deliverables: a defensible materials-science result and a maintainable reproducible software/data artifact. The most important question is not “Do you use AI?” but “Can I own a research-software/workflow component within an active materials question, and can that component be published or released responsibly?”

## Do not over-interpret

Public evidence cannot verify current capacity, interpersonal mentoring quality, compute allocation, IP/data restrictions, or admission probability. A Tier S recommendation is a reason to contact an advisor immediately, not an offer of supervision.
