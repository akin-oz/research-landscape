# FLEUR ecosystem vertical slice review

**Review date:** 2026-07-13

**Outcome:** adds two canonical entities and two evidence-bearing relationships:
ecosystem inclusion and Fortran implementation. Direct official sources support
the DFT/materials scope, MIT license, public source/build/test routes, and
open contribution context.

The slice deliberately does not create people, maintainer, institution, funder,
plugin, dependency, or user entities. A contribution invitation does not prove
access, acceptance, review authority, employment, support, mentoring,
supervision, availability, quality, or applicant outcomes.

Acceptance requires clean validation, deterministic generated outputs, and a
four-criterion FLEUR software-discovery regression. No schema or generator
change is introduced.
