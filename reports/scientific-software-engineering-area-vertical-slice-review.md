## Query review

The new group, PI, ecosystem, and direct-host university queries use existing
generic area-query kinds. No query-specific scoring, ranking, private-profile
input, or inferred relationship was introduced. Ecosystem and university
results are multi-hop displays of their existing sourced `connects`, `includes`,
or direct-host paths.

## Deliberate non-claims

1. The classification does not measure code quality, software maturity,
   contributor skill, career prospects, mentoring, funding, or admissions.
2. It does not mean every member of a classified group is an engineer or that
   every group output is software.
3. It does not create a taxonomy hierarchy or claim that adjacent research
   areas are subsets of Scientific Software Engineering.

## Verification record

The slice is accepted only when canonical validation, deterministic view and
recommendation drift checks, and regression tests pass after regeneration. It
introduces no architecture, schema, generator, or scoring change.
