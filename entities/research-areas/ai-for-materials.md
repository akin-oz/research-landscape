# AI for Materials

AI for Materials is a controlled research-area label for work explicitly
described as applying artificial intelligence to materials discovery or design.
It gives a reader a narrower discovery path than generic computational work
without claiming a universal subject taxonomy, research-quality distinction, or
equivalence with every data-driven materials project.

## Boundary and limitations

This label is used only where a record's own source directly connects
artificial intelligence and materials work. It is not a claim that every
materials-informatics, computational-materials, high-throughput, or software
project is an AI-for-Materials project, and it does not impose a broader/narrower
taxonomy on those adjacent controlled areas.
