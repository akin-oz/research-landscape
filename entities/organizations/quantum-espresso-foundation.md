# Quantum ESPRESSO Foundation

The Quantum ESPRESSO Foundation is represented as the public nonprofit
organization whose stated objectives include supporting, protecting, and
representing the Quantum ESPRESSO project and its user/developer communities.
It is distinct from the software suite and from all named representative
institutions.
