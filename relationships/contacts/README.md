# Contacts index

This directory is reserved for a future, deliberately minimal index of public professional contact routes. It is **not** an address book and must not contain private contact details, personal phone numbers, inferred availability, or information obtained outside lawful public sources.

## Source of truth

Each advisor’s interaction history lives in its own `relationships/active/<advisor-slug>/contact-log.md`. That local record is the source of truth for dates, messages, outcomes, and next actions. An index may link to an advisor record but must not duplicate its log.

## Permitted entries

Only record a route when it is either:

- published by the institution or the advisor for professional contact; or
- voluntarily supplied in a research interaction and appropriate to retain in this repository.

Store the route, its source URL or consent note, the access date, and a confidence label. Prefer a public institutional profile URL over copying an email address.

## Do not record

- private, personal, or third-party contact details;
- speculative availability, response habits, or social-media activity;
- content from private messages beyond the concise, consent-aware interaction notes needed by the relationship record;
- sensitive personal data or credentials.

If a contact becomes unsuitable to retain, remove it from the public working tree and use the project’s normal private-maintenance process rather than moving it into `archived/`.
