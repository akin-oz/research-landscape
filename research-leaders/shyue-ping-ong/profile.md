# Profile

UC San Diego lists Shyue Ping Ong as Professor of Chemical and Nano Engineering and describes his work as developing materials-informatics approaches, applying first-principles computation and other techniques to energy materials ([source](https://jacobsschool.ucsd.edu/people/profile/shyue-ping-ong)). Berkeley Lab’s Materials Project software page states that `pymatgen` is maintained by Prof. Ong at UC San Diego ([source](https://perssongroup.lbl.gov/software.html)).

**Current-status correction (2026-07-12):** [NUS](https://cde.nus.edu.sg/mse/staff/shyue-ping-ong/) identifies Ong as a Professor at the National University of Singapore who leads Materialyze.AI and is pymatgen's founder and lead developer. Use the [canonical PI record](../../entities/principal-investigators/shyue-ping-ong.md) for current public facts; the UCSD material above is historical context.
