# Group analysis

RIKEN’s team page publishes a current core-member list, research summary, annual reports and job-openings navigation ([source](https://www.r-ccs.riken.jp/en/research/labs/cmasrt/)). This is unusually useful public group context.

It does not document supervision quality, trainee outcomes, funding allocations, project ownership or a graduate affiliation. Treat those as open questions.
