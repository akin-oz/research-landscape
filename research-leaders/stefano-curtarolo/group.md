# Group analysis

AFLOW’s consortium page presents a distributed network of materials researchers; Duke lists current research interests and selected grants ([sources](https://www.aflow.org/consortium/), [Duke](https://mems.duke.edu/people/stefano-curtarolo/)).

Current student roster, group norms, funding allocation, alumni outcomes and openings are not assessed. Listed grants support programme activity but do not prove a funded student position.
