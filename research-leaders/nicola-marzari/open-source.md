# Open source

For this candidate, AiiDA and Materials Cloud are the central open-science interfaces: workflow provenance, plug-ins, HPC integration, archived data and reproducibility. Use the [AiiDA docs](https://aiida.readthedocs.io/) and [Materials Cloud](https://www.materialscloud.org/) to identify a bounded task.

Potential high-value work: plugin tests, provenance visualisation, data-archive validation or clear tutorials that connect a workflow to a physical calculation.
