# Research

The team describes building large polymer databases from experiment and simulation, developing foundation models, automating MD/DFT computational experiments, integrating simulation and experimental data through Sim2Real ML, and advancing AI/robotics-driven autonomous discovery ([source](https://www.riken.jp/en/research/labs/trip/agis/polymeromics/index.html)).

**Interpretation:** this is a high-confidence match for workflow/data/model engineering that remains tied to a clearly stated materials domain. The main fit question is whether the candidate wants polymer materials rather than inorganic/energy materials.
