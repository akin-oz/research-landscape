# Group analysis

The official team page names the Materials Software and Data Group as part of PSI’s Laboratory for Materials Simulations and describes AiiDA’s multi-institutional development structure ([source](https://www.aiida.net/sections/team.html)).

There is no dossier-level assessment of current students, group size, funding, outcomes or work practices. The historical software-engineer postdoc notice is evidence that such roles have existed, not that one exists now.
