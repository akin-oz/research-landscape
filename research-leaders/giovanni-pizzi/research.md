# Research

AiiDA publicly foregrounds workflows, provenance, plugin architecture, HPC interfaces, open science and open source ([source](https://www.aiida.net/sections/team.html)). Materials Cloud uses provenance-bearing computational data/workflows for sharing and reproducibility ([source](https://www.materialscloud.org/)).

**Interpretation:** the research interface is scientific workflow infrastructure that preserves how a result was produced. It is a strong fit for someone moving from engineering into computational materials, especially if they pair software work with a real simulation question.
