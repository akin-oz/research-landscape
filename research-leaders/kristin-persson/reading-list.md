# Reading and onboarding list

1. [Materials Project overview](https://perssongroup.lbl.gov/papers/jain-2018-mp.pdf) — platform and methods context.
2. [atomate paper record](https://eta-publications.lbl.gov/publications/atomate-high-level-interface-generate) — workflow design in materials simulations.
3. [Materials Project software catalogue](https://perssongroup.lbl.gov/software.html) — choose one maintained entry point.
4. [Materials Project documentation](https://docs.materialsproject.org/) — API/data-method conventions.

Do not contact the group claiming familiarity until you can reproduce one documented query or workflow and explain its scientific assumptions.
