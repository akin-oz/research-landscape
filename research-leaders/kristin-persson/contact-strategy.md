# Contact strategy

**Best timing:** after one narrow, public artifact: a `pymatgen`/`mp-api` contribution, reproducible Materials Project analysis, or a Materials Atlas interoperability prototype.

**Channel:** a concise, tailored email about a scientific/software intersection; use a project issue or pull request only for the relevant project contribution.

**Message shape:** one sentence on the specific group work; one link to a runnable artifact; one precise question about whether it intersects a current student/project need. Do not request a general career call or claim an unverified opening.
