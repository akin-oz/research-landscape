# Constitution

## Vision
Research Landscape is an evidence-based knowledge platform for discovering, evaluating and comparing global research ecosystems in Computational Materials Science, Scientific Software Engineering and AI for Materials.

## Mission
Help an experienced software engineer make better long-term academic decisions without relying on informal networks.

## Core Principles
- Evidence first.
- One canonical owner for every public fact.
- Architecture changes only through ADRs.
- Documentation is part of the product.
- Repository health is more important than repository size.
- Automation over manual repetition.
- Small vertical slices before scale.
- Facts and analysis are separate.
- Research ecosystems are more important than university prestige.
