# Principal-investigator records

This directory is the canonical PI index. The evidence-backed dossier records live in [`research-leaders/`](../research-leaders/); its first ten **anchor dossiers** are intentionally deeper than the global discovery slate and have public, first-party evidence of a relevant research-software/data/infrastructure interface.

The [global discovery slate](../reports/top-100-principal-investigators.md) lists 100 non-ranked candidates. It is a research queue, not a list of recommended supervisors. A record moves from discovery to anchor only when current role, research, software/open-science evidence and contact facts have been rechecked.

## Required dossier pack

Each anchor folder in `research-leaders/` uses the requested document set:

- `README.md` — scope, candidate fit and evidence boundary
- `profile.md` — role/biography only when primary-source supported
- `research.md` — research direction and evolution
- `group.md` — publicly evidenced group/lab characteristics
- `software.md` and `open-source.md` — distinct software-culture and community evidence
- `publications.md` — representative works interpreted through methods/data/software
- `mentorship.md` — evidence and unknowns; never a reputation score
- `reading-list.md` — only directly relevant reading/onboarding material
- `contact-strategy.md` — ethical, specific outreach plan
- `career-value.md` — applicant-specific interpretation with confidence

See the [source register](../reports/global-sources.md) and [evidence model](../docs/methodology/evidence-model.md) before adding a dossier.
