# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, professional, evidence-focused community. Participate in good faith; critique claims and methods, not people or institutions. Harassment, discrimination, doxxing, retaliation, and publishing private personal information are prohibited.

Report concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method in `SECURITY.md` or the repository host's reporting mechanisms. Maintainers may warn, edit, remove, or ban contributions to protect participants and the project.
