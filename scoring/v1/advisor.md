# Advisor scoring model v1

**Status:** Design contract; not yet operational.

Advisor compatibility is a vector of supported dimensions, not a rank. Each dimension has a 0–100 score only when its documented metrics, evidence coverage, and confidence requirements are met. Required output: model reference, inputs, formula, evidence IDs, coverage, confidence, unavailable dimensions, and sensitivity note.

Eligible dimensions are research alignment, publication activity, research quality, mentorship, open science, software engineering, scientific computing, international collaboration, industry collaboration, student outcomes, funding activity, and interdisciplinary work. Applicant weights are defined in [weights.md](weights.md).
