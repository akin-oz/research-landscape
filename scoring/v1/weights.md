# Default applicant weights v1

**Status:** Design contract; not yet operational.

These defaults are an optional baseline for a research-focused applicant, never a universal definition of quality. Weights total 100. Unavailable dimensions are excluded from the denominator and disclosed.

| Dimension | Weight | Rationale |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Research alignment | 20 | Direct fit shapes project and supervision opportunity. |
| Mentorship | 15 | Support structures materially affect graduate experience. |
| Research quality | 12 | Rigorous, field-aware research practice matters. |
| Publication activity | 10 | Activity signals opportunity, not quality by itself. |
| Open science | 8 | Transparency supports reproducibility. |
| Software engineering | 7 | Relevant to software-producing environments. |
| Scientific computing | 7 | Relevant to computational capability. |
| Student outcomes | 6 | Aggregate, contextual outcomes can inform expectations. |
| Funding activity | 5 | Supports feasibility and is field-dependent. |
| International collaboration | 4 | May broaden networks and exposure. |
| Industry collaboration | 3 | Relevant for applied pathways. |
| Interdisciplinary work | 3 | Relevant to cross-field research goals. |
