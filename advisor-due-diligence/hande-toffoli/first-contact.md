## Why this message is specific without overclaiming

- The two papers named are real, accessible, and technically relevant: [AIMD methane pyrolysis](https://doi.org/10.1021/acsaem.3c03235) and [LAMMPS tribology](https://doi.org/10.3390/lubricants12020046).
- It asks for advice rather than assumes capacity.
- It frames the applicant’s software experience as a possible contribution to a materials question, not as a request to make the advisor supervise a generic product.
- It does not claim the group uses Python, GitHub, ASE, pymatgen, or an open-source workflow; those remain questions.

## Attachments and links

Do **not** attach a long CV on first contact unless requested. Offer one link or one-page PDF only if it contains:

1. A 3–4 line profile and the long-term research direction.
2. One small reproducibility artifact (for example, a documented QE/LAMMPS exercise) with inputs, environment assumptions, expected result, and a clear README.
3. One concise architecture diagram showing how stable IDs, manifests, validations, and results would support a simulation campaign.
4. A statement of what is still being learned—DFT fundamentals, specific packages, or materials theory—rather than overstating competence.

## Follow-up protocol

| Timing | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Day 0 | Send the email on a weekday during normal working hours. |
| Day 8–10 | Send one short follow-up only: restate the research-advice framing and include a single updated example or ask whether another contact is more suitable. |
| No reply after ~3 weeks | Stop. Do not send repeated nudges; pursue other advisors while continuing the preparation work. |
| Positive reply | Ask for a short meeting and send the one-page brief. Use [meeting-questions.md](meeting-questions.md), but do not paste the question list into an email. |

## If a reply says “contact me when you apply”

Reply once, briefly:

> Thank you for the guidance. Before applying, would you recommend one paper, course, or small preparation exercise that would best help me assess fit with your current research? I will work through that and follow the formal process.

This preserves the relationship and converts a non-commitment into actionable preparation.
