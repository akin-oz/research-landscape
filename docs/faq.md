# Frequently asked questions

## Is this a ranking?

No. It produces evidence-backed compatibility profiles with configurable weights.

## Does a missing score mean poor performance?

No. It means evidence was insufficient or the metric was not applicable.

## Can an entity request a correction?

Yes. Open an evidence-correction issue with public sources, or use the maintainers' private channel for a safety or privacy concern.

## Are scores permanent?

No. They are versioned snapshots with evidence windows and may change when evidence or method changes.
