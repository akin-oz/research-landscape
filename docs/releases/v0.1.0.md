# Research Landscape v0.1.0 — Knowledge Model & Turkey Advisor Search

This release moves Research Landscape from repository foundation to an evidence-backed research knowledge system with its first real-world advisor analyses.

## Highlights

- Core knowledge model with entity and relationship contracts, stable identifiers, YAML frontmatter, JSON Schemas, and architectural decision records.
- Immutable `scoring/v1/` contracts that preserve compatibility-first interpretation and reproducibility.
- Akdeniz University pilot with roster reconciliation, advisor dossiers, evidence register, department comparison, and contact strategy.
- Nationwide Turkey shortlist of 20 potential MSc advisors across relevant research environments, with application tiers and source-backed caveats.

## Scope and limitations

The reports assess fit for a senior software engineer moving toward scientific software, computational materials, materials informatics, and AI for materials discovery. They do not rank scholars or universities by prestige, guarantee advisor availability, or replace direct conversations about supervision, data access, admissions, and project scope.

## Next milestone

Automation and structured validation tooling: reproducible source ingestion, schema/frontmatter checks, score-generation safeguards, and freshness monitoring.
