# Research groups

This namespace will hold group-level dossiers. Until every group is individually verified, use [the 50-group research queue](../reports/special-reports.md#50-research-groups-platforms-and-laboratories-to-investigate) and the [PI discovery slate](../reports/top-100-principal-investigators.md).

Group records should record public evidence for current people, research, funding, software, collaborations and vacancies. They must label unknown student outcomes, mentorship and culture rather than guessing.
